Half Day Adventure

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Class II – III
  • Price: $89 per person
  • Min. Age: 8 years

Perfect for first-timers and families, our Half Day Adventure takes you through scenic stretches of moderate white water with plenty of calm pools to enjoy the surrounding canyon views. Certified guides walk you through every paddle stroke before you hit the water. Lunch and gear included.

Big Mallard

  • Duration: Full Day (8 hours)
  • Difficulty: Class III – IV
  • Price: $149 per person
  • Min. Age: 14 years

The Big Mallard is one of the most iconic rapids on the Salmon River — a thundering Class IV drop that demands teamwork and trust. This full-day trip builds up to the main event with a series of Class III warm-up rapids, a riverside lunch break, and the unforgettable plunge through Big Mallard itself. Previous rafting experience recommended.

Book This Trip

Los Dientes del Diablo

  • Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • Difficulty: Class IV – V
  • Price: $499 per person
  • Min. Age: 18 years

"The Devil's Teeth" is our flagship expedition — a three-day journey through a remote wilderness canyon accessible only by river. You'll camp riverside under a canopy of stars, share meals cooked by our trail chefs, and tackle some of the most technically demanding white water in the western hemisphere. This is the trip serious paddlers dream about. Previous Class IV experience required.
