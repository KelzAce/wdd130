History

Rafting has a long and storied history, evolving from a necessity for transportation and trade to a beloved recreational activity. The roots of modern rafting can be traced back to the early explorers and traders who navigated wild rivers in their quest for new lands and resources.

Today, rafting is enjoyed by adventure seekers all over the world. From calm, scenic floats to adrenaline-pumping whitewater rapids, rafting offers something for everyone. Our rafting site celebrates this rich history and invites you to create your own adventure with us.
